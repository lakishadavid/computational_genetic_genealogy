JupyterLite for Computational Genetic Genealogy

Browser-Based Jupyter Environment

This is the JupyterLite environment for the Computational Genetic Genealogy course. It allows you to run Jupyter notebooks directly in your browser without any installation.

All computation happens locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology.

Launch JupyterLab Back to Course

Available Notebooks

  • Lab 1: Exploring Population Genomic Data

Important Notes

  • Your work is saved in your browser's storage. If you clear your browser data, your progress will be lost.
  • Use the "Download" button to save your work locally.
  • This environment has limitations compared to a full Jupyter installation, especially for large datasets.
  • Some Python packages may not be available or may have limited functionality.

Browser Compatibility

JupyterLite works best with recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari. If you encounter issues, try updating your browser.
