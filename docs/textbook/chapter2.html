Overview

This chapter guides you through setting up and mastering your computational environment for genetic genealogy analysis. You'll learn how to configure your development environment, use essential development tools, and work with specialized genetic analysis software. By the end of this chapter, you'll have a fully functional workspace ready for genetic genealogy research.
